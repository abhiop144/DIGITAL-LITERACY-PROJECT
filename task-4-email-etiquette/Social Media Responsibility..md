# 📱 Social Media Responsibility Checklist
**For:** CSE Students & Future Engineers

---

## ✅ The "DO'S" (Best Practices)

1. **Audit Your Privacy Settings:** Regularly check who can see your posts on platforms like Instagram and Facebook to keep your personal life private.
2. **Build a Professional Brand:** Use LinkedIn to share your project milestones and GitHub to host your "Cyber-Carnival" or AIML code.
3. **Fact-Check Before Sharing:** Verify information from multiple sources to avoid spreading AI-generated misinformation or fake news.
4. **Use Professional Photography:** Ensure your profile picture on professional sites is clear, high-quality, and appropriate for a recruiter to see.
5. **Network with Industry Experts:** Follow tech leaders and VIT alumni on X (Twitter) or Discord to stay updated on internship trends.

---

## ❌ The "DON'TS" (Common Mistakes)

1. **Don't Post Offensive Content:** Avoid sharing controversial or aggressive remarks that could be flagged during a corporate background check.
2. **Don't Share Real-Time Location:** Never post your exact hostel room number or live location data, as this poses a significant physical security risk.
3. **Don't Publicly Vent About University:** Avoid posting negative or unprofessional rants about professors or campus policies; keep those discussions to private channels.
4. **Don't Tag Others Without Consent:** Respect your batchmates' digital footprints—never tag them in unprofessional or embarrassing photos without asking.
5. **Don't Click Suspicious Links:** Avoid "Get Rich Quick" ads or phishing links that promise high returns, as these often target student data and fintech apps like Groww.
